1. Relational Engagement (Entry Level)

Practical tikanga, language, and engagement protocols for respectful relationships with Māori iwi, hapū and community stakeholders. Builds fluency and sustainable values-led partnerships through clear, staircased learning.

Audience: Non‑Māori team leaders, managers, HR
Format: Live online, 90 minutes
Minimum: 10 participants

2. Pōwhiri & Whakatau Readiness (Intermediate)

An intermediate session preparing confident practitioners to participate authentically in formal Māori welcome ceremonies. Covers pōwhiri flow, roles, responsibilities and practical participation readiness.

Audience: Non‑Māori team leaders, managers, HR
Format: Live online, 90 minutes
Minimum: 10 participants
Watch Video Book Now

3. Mana Motuhake in the Workplace (Advanced)

Advanced learning for experienced practitioners exploring self‑determination, autonomy and Indigenous authority in workplace settings. Deepens cultural confidence and leadership application.

Audience: Senior leaders, HR, People & Culture, SLT/ELT
Format: Live online, 90 minutes
Minimum: 10 participants
