About us

"Our Story, Our Team, Our Indigeneity."

Culturally Grounded Innovation for a Better Aotearoa

Blending Indigenous wisdom with modern tools to grow people, shift systems, and transform outcomes.

At Wolfgramm Holdings, we bring together kaupapa Māori and future-focused strategy to create real change in workplaces, research, and tourism.

Our work empowers teams, supports ethical research, builds digital confidence, and strengthens Indigenous-led innovation always grounded in whakapapa, whanaungatanga, and long-term impact.

Breviss Wolfgramm

Indigenous Strategist & Systems Innovator

Decades of leadership across sectors. A lifetime of cultural grounding.

Breviss Wolfgramm (Te Arawa, Ngāpuhi) is the founder of Wolfgramm Holdings, a respected Indigenous strategist, cultural advisor, and public speaker. With over 30 years of leadership across the military, hospitality, television, blockchain, and social services, he brings a rare blend of cultural depth and cross-sector insight to every space he enters.

Renowned for his ability to translate kaupapa Māori into tangible outcomes, Breviss is a trusted advisor to universities, iwi leaders, government agencies, and enterprise teams. His work bridges Indigenous wisdom with modern innovation helping organisations lead with integrity, build authentic partnerships, and embed systems change that lasts.

Five Pillars. One Purpose: Culturally Grounded Innovation.

Our work spans artificial intelligence, cultural transformation, immersive tourism, academic partnerships, and strategic systems change — all underpinned by Māori values.

AI & Automation for Business

We help SMEs future-proof their operational efficiencies with AI workshops, prompt engineering, and custom workflow automation. From time-saving systems to powerful marketing tools. Our solutions unlock digital capability with cultural care.

Cultural Competency & Te Tiriti Workshops

We deliver high-trust cultural training for boards, teams, and leaders. These include Te Tiriti education, relational leadership programmes, and immersive Marae experiences to transform workplace culture from the inside out.

Māori Tourism Experiences

Through our Marae Day and cultural immersion offerings, we help international and domestic visitors experience the living heartbeat of Māori Aotearoa — with excellence, authenticity, and manaakitanga.

Relational Research Partnerships

We support PhD researchers and academic institutions to connect meaningfully with tikanga Māori. Our engagement includes kaupapa Māori methodology training, co-design, and relationship brokering with Māori providers and communities.

Strategic Engagement & Systems Change

From MOUs to social procurement frameworks, we help organisations build authentic, long-term partnerships with iwi, hapū, and Māori-led providers. Our work drives equity, trust, and innovation across systems.

Relational First. Outcomes Second.

We don't just deliver services, we guide transformation. Every partnership begins with whakawhanaungatanga and is shaped through genuine connection, cultural integrity, and co-design.

At Wolfgramm Holdings, impact doesn't start with a product it starts with people. Our approach is grounded in tikanga Māori and tailored to each kaupapa. We listen deeply, build trust early, and design each engagement with long-term benefit in mind.

Whether it's introducing AI to business owners, facilitating Marae Day experiences, or supporting PhD researchers to embed kaupapa Māori into their methodologies, we take a relational path that leads to durable, measurable outcomes.

Our process is guided by:

Cultural authenticity – not performative, but grounded.

Shared accountability – we stay connected to ensure sustained growth.
