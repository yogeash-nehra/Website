Our Services

"Culturally Grounded Solutions for Modern Transformation"

Five Pillars of Indigenous-Led Innovation

We work across multiple sectors to deliver customised culturally grounded transformation.

Our services span five core areas — each grounded in Māori knowledge, relational integrity, and future-focused practice. From immersive cultural tourism to AI automation, from Te Tiriti training to leadership wellbeing programmes, we help organisations embed Indigenous values into everyday practice.

Every service is designed with care, co-designed with purpose, and delivered with excellence. We don't offer one-size-fits-all solutions — we listen, strategise, and walk alongside you.

Indigenous Tourism

Authentic Māori Cultural Experiences in Auckland

Experience Indigenous culture through a full-day immersive journey at a traditional tribal meeting ground in Auckland. Designed for groups and conference delegates, it offers authentic connection through ceremony, storytelling, and hands-on cultural engagement.

Our Marae Day experiences are more than tourism — they're transformational. Visitors participate in pōwhiri (welcoming ceremony), learn waiata (songs), engage with taonga (cultural treasures), and share kai (food) in a setting of genuine hospitality and manaakitanga.

What's Included:

• Full-day immersive Marae experience

• Traditional pōwhiri welcome ceremony

• Cultural workshops and storytelling

• Authentic kai (traditional meal)

• Guided tours and cultural activities

Relational Engagement for Non-Māori Organisations

Te Tiriti Education & Cultural Capability Building

Te Tiriti education, cultural readiness assessments, and tailored workshops to embed equity, understanding, and responsiveness into your team. Our programmes help non-Māori organisations build genuine relationships with Māori communities and embed Treaty principles into practice.

We deliver everything from foundational tikanga workshops to advanced Treaty training, designed for boards, leadership teams, and frontline staff. Each session is contextualised to your sector and challenges, ensuring learning translates into meaningful action.

Our Workshops Include:

• Te Tiriti o Waitangi foundations

• Tikanga, kawa, and cultural protocols

• Preparing for pōwhiri and whakatau

• Mana Motuhake and self-determination

• Cultural readiness assessments

Leadership & Wellbeing

Twelve-week programmes and wellbeing strategies that build culturally grounded, resilient leaders aligned with Indigenous values. Our leadership development approach integrates Māori concepts of mana, whakapapa, and collective responsibility.

Whether you're developing emerging leaders or supporting executive teams, our programmes foster self-awareness, cultural intelligence, and sustainable wellbeing. We blend contemporary leadership frameworks with tikanga Māori to create holistic, transformative learning experiences.

Programme Features:

Indigenous Systems Designer

Kaupapa Māori Research & Academic Partnerships

We help PhD researchers and providers embed tikanga into research and practice through onboarding, workshops, and co-designed Memorandum of Understanding. Our work ensures research is culturally safe, relationally grounded, and beneficial to Māori communities.

From methodology development to community engagement protocols, we support academics and institutions to navigate kaupapa Māori research with integrity. We broker relationships, facilitate hui, and provide ongoing cultural supervision throughout the research journey.

Research Support Includes:

• Kaupapa Māori methodology training

• Tikanga-based research protocols

• Community relationship brokering

• Co-designed MOUs and agreements

• Cultural supervision and guidance

How We Work With You

Every engagement with Wolfgramm Holdings begins with whakawhanaungatanga — building connection and trust. We don't just deliver services; we co-design solutions that reflect your context, goals, and cultural readiness.

Our Process:

1. Kōrero (Conversation)

We start with listening. A free strategy session helps us understand your needs, challenges, and aspirations.

2. Co-Design

Based on what we hear, we develop a tailored approach — whether that's a one-day workshop, a 12-week programme, or ongoing partnership.

3. Delivery

We bring expertise, care, and cultural authenticity to every session. Our facilitation is engaging, practical, and transformative.

4. Follow-Up & Support

Transformation doesn't end when the workshop does. We stay connected to ensure learning is embedded and outcomes are sustained.
