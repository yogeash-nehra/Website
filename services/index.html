What We Offer

Five pillars. One purpose: culturally grounded innovation.

Our services are grouped into five core pillars — each designed to unlock transformation in your team, community, or organisation through powerful, indigenous-led experiences.

Artificial Intelligence & Workflow Automation

We help organisations adopt artificial intelligence and workflow automation to streamline operations, enhance impact, and future-proof performance — all while remaining grounded in people, values, and whanaungatanga.

AI and Operational Efficiencies

Streamline your workflows, reduce manual labour, and boost productivity using tailored AI tools designed around your business needs.

Agentic AI Training for Enterprise Organisations

Hands-on sessions that equip leaders and business owners to automate key functions, enhance client engagement, and scale operations with confidence.

AI Maturity Audits

Assess where your organisation sits on the AI readiness spectrum and receive a customised roadmap for strategic, ethical, and impactful adoption.

Staff and Community Capability Training

Upskill your team or community to confidently adopt and use AI through culturally relevant training that enhances day-to-day performance.

Understanding Technology & AI: Entry Level Workshops

A practical introduction to AI for Māori youth, whānau, and small businesses covering what AI is, how it works, and where it adds real-world value.

Cultural Competency & Te Tiriti Training

We help organisations build deeper cultural intelligence and embed authentic, values-based practices through immersive training, Te Tiriti education, and Māori-led frameworks.

Te Tiriti Workshops

Interactive, values-based sessions for leaders and teams to unpack Te Tiriti principles and apply them to workplace culture and decision-making.

Immersive Marae Day

A full-day, on-marae experience where teams engage with tikanga, kōrero tuku iho (oral traditions), and Māori-led protocols to foster deep understanding and connection.

Māori Language Revitalisation Classes

Personalised reo Māori sessions for executives and teams that build fluency, confidence, and everyday application in workplace contexts.

Cultural Workshops & Traditional Practices

Hands-on modules in poi, weaving, haka, and more, designed to embed mātauranga Māori and enhance cultural fluency across teams.

Module-Based Leadership Training

Structured 12-week leadership development grounded in Māori values, with weekly workshops, quizzes, and reflective tools to build culturally intelligent leaders.

Cultural Tourism & Immersive Experiences

We create immersive, authentic Māori experiences that connect manuhiri (visitors) with te ao Māori through storytelling, performance, and genuine engagement designed to uplift local economies and share the living culture of Aotearoa.

Marae Day Immersive Experience

A fully guided marae-based experience designed for domestic and international guests. Includes pōwhiri, kai, kōrero, and hands-on activities that build connection, understanding, and cultural appreciation.

Māori Cultural Performance & Storytelling

Offer bespoke kapa haka, waiata, and traditional storytelling sessions — tailored for conferences, educational institutions, and tourism operators seeking deeper engagement.

Bespoke Cultural Itinerary Design

Co-create full-day or multi-day experiences for tour groups, schools, or corporate groups — weaving together Māori kai, art, performance, and ancestral sites with local tangata whenua hosts.

Tourism Capability Building for Māori Operators

Support Māori tourism providers to develop digital assets, AI-enhanced booking systems, and marketing pathways that highlight cultural identity and elevate customer experience.

WH Partnerships with Auckland Council (Tātaki Auckland Unlimited)

Proud to be selected as one of a handful of Māori-owned operators supported through Auckland's tourism development fund — delivering high-quality, whānau-led visitor experiences with economic impact.

Leadership & Wellbeing

We grow leaders who are culturally grounded, values-led, and future-ready. Our leadership and wellbeing programmes build confidence, cohesion, and clarity, empowering teams to navigate complexity with resilience, purpose, and relational depth.

12-Week Cultural Leadership Programme

A guided programme for senior leaders focused on values-led strategy, relational practice, and Māori-led leadership frameworks.

Strategic Wellbeing Modules

Interactive modules designed to support holistic wellbeing, aligned with kaupapa Māori models and team transformation.

Whānau-Centric Leadership Coaching

1:1 and small group coaching for executives and team leads — grounded in te ao Māori and focused on long-term impact.

Leadership Reflection & Cultural Maturity Dashboards

Track team growth with real-time dashboards that measure cultural fluency, leadership behaviours, and wellbeing alignment.

On-Site Cultural Immersion (e.g, Hāngī on Site)

Transform the workplace into a living learning space including on-site hāngī, waiata, karakia, and board engagement.

Relational Research & Indigenous Engagement

We support research teams, health agencies, and tertiary institutions to engage with Māori communities in ways that are culturally grounded, relationally accountable, and co-designed for long-term impact — not just data extraction or compliance.

Tikanga-Based Research Onboarding

Support for institutions to onboard researchers using tikanga-led processes that centre whanaungatanga and cultural safety.

Māori-Centered Co-Design Frameworks

Facilitated design sprints, wānanga, and MOU development rooted in mana-enhancing partnership models.

Provider-Facing Relational Workshops

Trainings that deepen providers' understanding of Māori worldviews, helping them navigate complex cultural landscapes with care and competence.

Cultural Intelligence Dashboards

Tools to assess and improve relational maturity across organisations, with a focus on values-alignment and cultural readiness.

Kaupapa Māori Research Partnerships

Strategic alliances that enable joint research grounded in mātauranga Māori, ethical integrity, and long-term community impact.

Let's Build Something Meaningful Together

Whether you're growing your business, leading a research project, or building cultural capability, we're here to help you move with purpose and integrity.
